## Question

How do I log in and begin using State Affairs?

## Answer

1. Visit `https://stateaffairs.com` in your browser.

2. Click the "Login" button at the top right.

3. Enter your organization's email and password.

4. If you forgot your password, click "Forgot Password" to reset it.

5. Once logged in, you can view, edit, and organize all your documentation projects from the dashboard.

<Callout kind="tip" collapsed="false">
  Need access? Contact your admin to be added to your State Affairs workspace.
</Callout>
