---
title: Cannot View Documents
description: Resolve document access issues in State Affairs.
---

## Symptoms

- "Access Denied" error when opening a document
- Documents not visible in your workspace

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check permissions">
    Ensure you are logged in with the correct user account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request document access">
    Click "Request Access" or contact your project admin for permissions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh workspace">
    Log out and log back in. If you still cannot access, clear your browser cache and retry.
  </Step>
</Steps>
