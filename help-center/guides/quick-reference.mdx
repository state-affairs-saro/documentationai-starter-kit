## ğŸ¯ Most Common Tasks

### I need to update my credit card
1. Go to **Account Overview** tab
2. Click **"Payment Portal"** button
3. Update payment method
4. Save changes

---

### I need to add a new team member
1. Go to **Team Members** tab
2. Click **"+ Invite Member"** button
3. Enter their email and details
4. Assign them a seat
5. Click **"Send Invitation"**

---

### Someone left the company - remove their access
1. Go to **Team Members** tab
2. Find the person
3. Click **three-dot menu** (Actions)
4. Click **"Deactivate"**
5. Confirm

---

### I'm getting too many emails
1. Go to **Profile Settings** tab
2. **Uncheck** "Real-time notifications"
3. Keep **one** Daily notification time
4. You'll now get 1 email/day instead of many

---

### I need to download an invoice
1. Go to **Account Overview** tab
2. Click **"View Past Invoices"**
3. Find your invoice
4. Click download/PDF icon

---

### When am I being charged next?
1. Go to **Account Overview** tab
2. Look at top: "Your next invoice will be issued on [Date]"

---

### How do I change my password?
1. Go to **Profile Settings** tab
2. Scroll to bottom
3. Click **"Change Password"** button
4. Enter current + new password
5. Save and log back in

---

### How do I make someone an Admin?
1. Go to **Team Members** tab
2. Find the person
3. Click their **"Role"** dropdown
4. Select **"Admin"**
5. They now have admin access

---

### How do I create a label for organizing bills?
1. Go to **Label Settings** tab
2. Click **"+ Add Label"** button
3. Name your label
4. Save
5. Use it on bill pages

---

### How do I request more seats?
1. Go to **Account Overview** tab
2. Scroll to "Add additional seats"
3. Click **"Request Seat"** under your desired tier
4. Fill out the form
5. Our team will contact you
