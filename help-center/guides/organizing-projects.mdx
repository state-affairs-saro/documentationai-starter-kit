## Step-by-Step Instructions

### 1. Navigate to Reports
Go to the **Reports** section from the main navigation menu.

### 2. Access Daily Update
Click on **"View Report"** under the Daily Update section to see bills updated or introduced today.

### 3. Select Custom Date
To view bills from a specific date, click the date picker in the Daily Update view.

### 4. Choose Date Range  
Select your desired date range using the calendar interface. You can choose:
- Single day
- Date range (from/to)
- Preset ranges (Last 7 days, Last 30 days, etc.)

### 5. Apply Filters
Click **"Apply"** to filter bills based on your selected dates.

## Common Use Cases

**Track Today's Activity:**
Use the Daily Update to see all new bills and updates from today.

**Weekly Review:**
Set date range to "Last 7 Days" to review the week's legislative activity.

**Specific Event Analysis:**
Filter to a specific date range when analyzing bills around committee hearings or floor votes.
