## Overview

Keep your documentation organized by grouping work into projects.

## How To

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new project">
    Click "New Project" on your dashboard and enter the project name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add documents">
    Inside the new project, click "Add Document" to upload or create content.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Organize with folders">
    Use "New Folder" to separate documentation by topic or team.
  </Step>
</Steps>
