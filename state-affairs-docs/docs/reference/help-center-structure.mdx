# Help Center Structure

# State Affairs Help Center - Structure & Navigation

## Overview
This document outlines the complete structure for the State Affairs Help Center, organized by customer journey and common inquiry types.

---

## Main Navigation Categories

### 1. Getting Started
*For new customers and first-time users*

#### Articles:
- **Welcome to State Affairs** - Platform overview
- **Requesting Access Credentials** - How to get your account set up
- **Setting Up Your Account** - First-time login and profile setup
- **Navigating the Platform** - Tour of key features
- **Setting Up Your First Keyword Alert** - Quick start guide
- **Mobile Access Guide** - Using State Affairs on your phone/tablet

---

### 2. Account Management
*Managing users, settings, and preferences*

#### Articles:
- **Adding Team Members to Your Account** - User management
- **Managing Notification Preferences** - Customizing email alerts
- **Password Reset & Account Security** - Login troubleshooting
- **Updating Your Contact Information** - Email and profile updates
- **Account Settings Overview** - All available settings explained
- **Two-Factor Authentication Setup** - Enhanced security (if available)

---

### 3. Billing & Subscriptions
*Payment, invoices, and subscription management*

#### Articles:
- **Understanding Your Subscription** - What's included in your plan
- **Billing & Invoice Issues** - Comprehensive troubleshooting guide
- **How Autopay Works** - Automatic payment setup and management
- **Changing Your Subscription** - Upgrading, downgrading, or modifying
- **Cancellation Policy** - How to cancel and what to expect
- **Understanding Multiple Subscriptions** - Print vs. Digital options
- **Invoice Questions & Clarifications** - Common billing inquiries
- **Payment Methods** - Accepted payment types and updating payment info

---

### 4. Platform Features
*Using the enhanced content platform*

#### Articles:
- **Keyword Alerts Setup & Management** - Complete guide
- **Advanced Search Features** - Finding archived content
- **Customizing Your News Feed** - Personalization options
- **Saving & Organizing Articles** - Bookmarking and collections
- **Sharing Content** - How to share articles with colleagues
- **Printing & Exporting** - Downloading content
- **Understanding Content Categories** - Navigation by topic
- **Real-Time Notifications** - How breaking news alerts work

---

### 5. Content & Delivery
*Print subscriptions and content access*

#### Articles:
- **Print Delivery Information** - What to expect with print subscriptions
- **Missing or Late Deliveries** - How to report issues
- **Changing Your Delivery Address** - Mailing address updates
- **Duplicate Delivery Issues** - Resolving multiple copies
- **Content Update Schedule** - When new content is published
- **Accessing Historical Content** - Archive navigation
- **Content Types Explained** - News, analysis, special reports

---

### 6. Technical Support
*Troubleshooting technical issues*

#### Articles:
- **Supported Browsers & Devices** - Technical requirements
- **Login Issues & Troubleshooting** - Can't access your account
- **Platform Performance Issues** - Slow loading, errors
- **Mobile App Download & Setup** - iOS and Android apps
- **Browser Cache & Cookies** - Clearing for better performance
- **Common Error Messages** - What they mean and how to fix them
- **Accessibility Features** - Screen readers and accessibility tools

---

### 7. State-Specific Help
*Organized by publication/state*

#### Sub-categories by State:
- **Arizona** (Arizona Capitol Times)
  - Arizona-specific features
  - Local delivery information
  - Arizona legislative coverage

- **Massachusetts** (State House News Service)
  - Enhanced content platform for MA
  - Massachusetts-specific features
  - MA legislative session coverage

- **Multi-State**
  - Cross-state coverage
  - National policy tracking
  - Multi-jurisdiction subscriptions

## Help Center Analytics

### Track These Metrics:
1. Most viewed articles
2. Search terms with no results
3. Articles with highest "Was this helpful? No" ratings
4. Time spent on each article
5. Customer journey through help center before contacting support

### Use Data To:
- Identify gaps in documentation
- Improve existing articles
- Create new content for common issues
- Optimize search functionality
- Reduce support ticket volume

---

## Content Maintenance Schedule

### Weekly:
- Review new support tickets for emerging issues
- Update any outdated information
- Add new FAQ entries based on customer questions

### Monthly:
- Analyze help center metrics
- Update screenshots if platform changes
- Review and refresh top 10 most-viewed articles
- Check all links for broken URLs

### Quarterly:
- Major content audit
- Rewrite underperforming articles
- Add new features documentation
- Review entire structure for improvements
- Survey customers about help center usefulness
