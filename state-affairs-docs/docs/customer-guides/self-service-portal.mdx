# Self-Service Portal

# State Affairs Self-Service Portal Guide

## Overview

The State Affairs self-service portal allows you to manage your account, subscriptions, team members, and preferences without needing to contact support. Access the portal anytime at **Settings** (click your profile icon in the top right).

**Portal URL:** https://pro.stateaffairs.com/settings/account

---

## Quick Navigation

The portal has 4 main sections:
1. **Account Overview** - Billing, invoices, and subscription management
2. **Team Members** - Manage users and permissions
3. **Profile Settings** - Notification preferences and password
4. **Label Settings** - Create and manage custom labels for bills

## Managing Your Seats

### What is a "Seat"?

A seat is one user license for State Affairs. Each seat:
- Is assigned to one team member
- Provides access to specific states
- Has a specific tier (News or Pro)
- Cannot be shared between multiple people

---

### Viewing Your Current Seats

**What you'll see:**

| Seat Type | State | Seats | Action |
|-----------|-------|-------|--------|
| Pro | Indiana | 1 | Reassign Seats |

**This tells you:**
- **Seat Type:** The tier/level of service (Pro, News, or Enterprise)
- **State:** Which state legislature this seat covers
- **Seats:** How many licenses you have
- **Action:** Options to reassign

---

### Reassigning Seats

**When to use this:**  
When someone leaves your organization or changes roles, you can reassign their seat to someone else without losing access.

**How to reassign:**
1. Click "Reassign Seats" button
2. Select the current user
3. Choose the new user from your team
4. Confirm the reassignment

**Important:** The original user will immediately lose access and the new user will gain access.

---

### Adding More Seats

**Two ways to add seats:**

#### Option 1: Request a Specific Tier
Scroll down to "Add additional seats" section.

You'll see three tiers:

**News Seat** - From $600/seat
- State-level political news
- Exclusive, nonpartisan coverage
- Policy & issue tracking
- Real-time notifications
- Mobile app access

**Pro Seat** - $2,250/seat
- Everything in News, plus:
- AI-analysis for bills & hearings
- Searchable hearing transcripts
- Real-time bill tracking & reports
- Directories & voting patterns

**Enterprise** - Custom pricing
- Everything in Pro, plus:
- 50-state coverage
- AI-assisted relevance scores
- Custom AI prompts
- Dedicated account manager

**To request:**  
Click "Request Seat" under the tier you want.

#### Option 2: General Request
Click "Add seat/state" button at the bottom.

**What happens next:**
1. A form opens for you to specify your needs
2. Our team reviews your request
3. You receive a quote via email
4. Accept the quote to add seats

**Response time:** Typically within 24 hours

## Understanding Team Member Roles

### Admin
**What they can do:**
- Everything a Member can do, PLUS:
- Invite new team members
- Remove team members
- Change user roles
- Assign/reassign seats
- Access billing information
- Manage account settings

**Who should be Admin:**
- Account owner
- IT administrators
- Office managers
- Anyone responsible for user management

---

### Member
**What they can do:**
- Access State Affairs platform
- Use bill tracking features
- Set keyword alerts
- Create reports
- Collaborate with team

**What they CANNOT do:**
- Invite other users
- Change permissions
- Access billing
- Manage account settings

**Who should be Member:**
- Regular users
- Analysts
- Policy researchers
- Anyone who just needs to use the platform

## Inviting New Team Members

### Step-by-Step Process

1. **Click "+ Invite Member" button** (top right, blue button)

2. **Fill out invitation form:**
   - Email address (required)
   - First name
   - Last name
   - Role (Admin or Member)
   - Seat assignment (which state/tier)

3. **Click "Send Invitation"**

4. **What happens next:**
   - User receives invitation email
   - They click link in email
   - They create password
   - They gain immediate access

**Important notes:**
- You must have an available seat to assign
- If all seats are in use, request additional seats first
- Invitation emails expire after 7 days
- Expired invitations can be resent

## Notification Management

State Affairs has two types of notifications:
1. **Bill Updates** - When bills change status, get amended, etc.
2. **Keywords** - When content matches your saved keywords

For each type, you can control:
- Real-time notifications (immediate alerts)
- Daily digest timing (batched summary)

---

### Bill Updates Notifications

#### Real-time notifications (checkbox)

**When CHECKED (enabled):**
- You get immediate email when a tracked bill updates
- Email sent within minutes of the change
- Good for: Time-sensitive bills, active legislative sessions

**When UNCHECKED (disabled):**
- You don't get immediate alerts
- Changes are only included in daily digest
- Good for: Less urgent tracking, reducing email volume

---

#### Daily notifications (EST)

Choose when you want your daily summary:

**Options:**
- ☐ None - No daily digest
- ☐ 6 AM - Early morning, before work
- ☑ 12 PM - Lunchtime (most popular)
- ☐ 6 PM - End of workday

**What's in the daily digest:**
- Summary of all bill updates from the past 24 hours
- Organized by bill
- Includes bills from your tracked lists

**Recommended setting:** 12 PM if you check during lunch, or 6 AM if you want to start your day informed.

---

### Keyword Notifications

Same settings as Bill Updates:

#### Real-time notifications (checkbox)
**When CHECKED:**
- Immediate email when content matches your keywords
- Could be multiple emails per day if keywords are broad

**When UNCHECKED:**
- Keywords only appear in daily digest
- Better for broad keywords that match frequently

---

#### Daily notifications (EST)

Same timing options as Bill Updates.

**Pro tip:** Many users set Keywords and Bills to the same time so everything arrives in one digest.

## Change Password

**When to change your password:**
- You think your account may be compromised
- It's been 6+ months
- Company security policy requires it
- You shared it and want to revoke access

**How to change:**

1. **Click "Change Password" button** (blue button at bottom)

2. **You'll be prompted for:**
   - Current password
   - New password
   - Confirm new password

3. **Password requirements:**
   - At least 8 characters (longer is better!)
   - Mix of letters and numbers recommended
   - Special characters recommended
   - Don't reuse old passwords

4. **Click "Save" or "Update"**

5. **You'll be logged out** and need to log back in with the new password

**Security tips:**
- Use a password manager
- Don't share your password
- Use a unique password (not used on other sites)
- Don't write it down

**Forgot your current password?**  
Log out and use "Forgot Password" on the login screen.

## Common Profile Settings Tasks

### How to: Stop Getting So Many Emails

**Problem:** Too many notification emails

**Solution:**
1. Go to Profile Settings
2. Uncheck "Real-time notifications" for Bills and/or Keywords
3. Set one Daily notification time (like 12 PM)
4. Now you'll get 1 email per day instead of many

---

### How to: Get Immediate Alerts for Important Bills

**Problem:** You need to know about changes as they happen

**Solution:**
1. Go to Profile Settings
2. Check "Real-time notifications" under Bill Updates
3. Keep Daily notifications enabled too as backup
4. You'll now get immediate + daily summary

---

### How to: Change Your Daily Summary Time

**Problem:** Emails arrive at inconvenient times

**Solution:**
1. Go to Profile Settings
2. Under "Daily notifications (EST)", select your preferred time
3. Choose when you actually check your email
4. Changes take effect the next day

**Popular choices:**
- 6 AM - for early birds
- 12 PM - for lunch checkers
- 6 PM - for end-of-day reviewers

---

### How to: Make a Different State Your Homepage

**Problem:** You track multiple states but want to see one first

**Solution:**
1. Go to Profile Settings
2. Scroll to "Default State"
3. Select your preferred state from dropdown
4. Next time you log in, you'll start there

---

### How to: Change Your Password

1. Go to Profile Settings
2. Scroll to bottom
3. Click "Change Password"
4. Enter current password
5. Enter new password twice
6. Save
7. Log back in with new password

## What Are Labels?

Labels are custom tags you create to organize bills. Think of them like folders or categories that you define.

**Examples of labels:**
- "Priority Bills" - Your team's top-priority tracking
- "Education Issues" - All education-related bills
- "Budget Impact" - Bills affecting your budget
- "Committee Review" - Bills your committee is reviewing
- "Watch List" - Bills to keep an eye on
- "Support" - Bills your organization supports
- "Oppose" - Bills your organization opposes

---

## What You Can Do Here

✅ Create new labels  
✅ View all labels  
✅ See which bills are tagged with each label  
✅ See which team members are using labels  
✅ Reorder labels (drag and drop)  
✅ Delete labels  
✅ Search labels
