# Frequently Asked Questions

# State Affairs Support - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## Account & Access

### How do I get access to the enhanced content platform?
Email statehousenews@stateaffairs.com or support@stateaffairs.com to request credentials. Include your name, organization, and email address. You'll receive login instructions within 24 hours.

### I didn't receive my login credentials. What should I do?
1. Check your spam/junk folder
2. Wait 24 hours from your initial request
3. If still missing, contact support@stateaffairs.com

### How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the email instructions. If you don't receive the reset email, check spam or contact support.

### Can I add team members to my account?
Yes! Email support@stateaffairs.com with the names and email addresses of colleagues you'd like to add. We'll send them setup instructions.

### What browsers are supported?
Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge are all supported. Chrome is recommended for the best experience.

## Billing & Subscriptions

### Why was I charged multiple times?
This can happen if:
- You have multiple active subscriptions (e.g., print+digital AND digital-only)
- Our system caught up on previously missed autopay charges
- There was a billing system migration issue

Contact billing@stateaffairs.com for a detailed invoice breakdown.

### How do I cancel a subscription?
Email billing@stateaffairs.com with the subscription you want to cancel. Specify which subscription to keep if you have multiple.

### Do I have multiple subscriptions?
If you're unsure, contact billing@stateaffairs.com. We'll review your account and let you know which subscriptions are active.

### What's your refund policy?
For cancelled subscriptions, we typically write off outstanding balances rather than refund previously collected payments. Contact billing@stateaffairs.com for specific refund requests.

### I'm receiving duplicate newspapers. How do I fix this?
Email support@stateaffairs.com with your mailing address and how many copies you're receiving. We'll contact the printer to remove the duplicate.

### How does autopay work?
Once enrolled, your payment method will be automatically charged each billing period. You'll receive invoices via email for your records.

### I have autopay but still received a past due notice. Why?
Contact billing@stateaffairs.com to verify your autopay status and payment method on file. We'll ensure the system is properly configured.

## Content & Features

### How do I set up keyword alerts?
1. Log in to your account
2. Go to Settings or Alerts
3. Click "Add Keyword Alert"
4. Enter your terms and save

If you have trouble, contact support@stateaffairs.com and we can set them up manually.

### My keyword alerts disappeared. What happened?
This can happen after system updates. Log out and back in, then re-add your keywords. If they continue to disappear, contact support.

### Can I receive ALL stories instead of just keyword alerts?
Yes! Contact support@stateaffairs.com and we'll adjust your notification settings to send you every story as it's published.

### How do I search archived content?
Use the search function in your enhanced content account. You can search by keyword, date, author, or topic.

### What's included in my subscription?
Depending on your plan:
- **Digital Only**: Online access to all content, keyword alerts, search functionality
- **Print + Digital**: Everything in digital plus printed newspaper delivery
- **Enhanced Platform**: Real-time updates, advanced search, mobile access, team management

Contact your account representative for details about your specific subscription.

## Technical Issues

### The platform is running slowly. What should I do?
1. Clear your browser cache
2. Try a different browser
3. Check your internet connection
4. If issues persist, contact support@stateaffairs.com

### I'm getting error messages when trying to log in.
1. Verify you're using the correct email and password
2. Try resetting your password
3. Clear browser cookies
4. Try a different browser
5. Contact support@stateaffairs.com if the problem continues

### Can I access content on my phone or tablet?
Yes! The platform is fully responsive. Simply visit the website in your mobile browser and log in.

### Is there a mobile app?
Yes! State Affairs has mobile apps available for:
- iOS: Download from the App Store
- Android: Download from Google Play

Search for "State Affairs" in your app store.

## Delivery & Content Issues

### I didn't receive my newspaper this week.
Email support@stateaffairs.com with:
- Your mailing address
- Which issue(s) you're missing
- When you last received a copy

We can have missed issues sent to you.

### Can I change my delivery address?
Yes! Email support@stateaffairs.com with your new mailing address at least one week before you need the change to take effect.

### How often is content published?
- **Daily publications**: New content posted throughout each business day
- **Weekly publications**: Published according to your specific publication's schedule
- **Breaking news**: Posted immediately as stories develop

### I'm seeing old content on the homepage. How do I see the latest?
1. Refresh your browser
2. Clear your cache
3. Check the date filter settings
4. Ensure you're on the main news feed, not a saved search

## Privacy & Security

### Who has access to my account information?
Only State Affairs staff members who need access to provide support. We never share your information with third parties.

### Can I make my account more secure?
Yes! Use a strong, unique password and consider using a password manager. Enable two-factor authentication if available in your account settings.

### What information do you collect?
We collect email addresses, names, organization information, and usage data to improve our service. See our privacy policy for full details.

## Sales & Account Escalations

### I have a complaint about a sales representative.
Email support@stateaffairs.com and request to speak with a senior executive. Include details about the issue. We'll connect you with our Chief Revenue Officer or VP of Sales.

### I need to speak with someone in management.
For escalated issues, contact support@stateaffairs.com and specify that you need management involvement. We'll ensure the right person contacts you.

### How do I update my payment method?
Email billing@stateaffairs.com with your request. For security, we'll guide you through the process rather than collecting payment information via email.
