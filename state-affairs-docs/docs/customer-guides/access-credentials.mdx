# Access & Credentials

# Getting Access to Enhanced Content

## Overview
This guide walks you through requesting and activating your credentials for the enhanced State Affairs platform experience.

## Requesting Access Credentials

### First Time Access

If you're a new subscriber or upgrading to the enhanced platform:

1. **Send Your Request**
   - Email: statehousenews@stateaffairs.com (for State House News Service)
   - Email: support@stateaffairs.com (for other State Affairs products)
   - Subject: "Request for Enhanced Content Credentials"

2. **Include in Your Email:**
   - Your full name
   - Organization name
   - Email address you want associated with the account
   - Which publication(s) you subscribe to

3. **What Happens Next:**
   - You'll receive login instructions via email within 24 hours
   - Check your spam/junk folder if you don't see it in your inbox
   - The email will contain a link to set up your account

### Activating Your Account

Once you receive the credentials email:

1. Click the activation link in the email
2. Follow the prompts to create your password
3. Complete your profile setup
4. You'll immediately have access to your enhanced content

**Having Trouble?**
- Check spam/junk folders for the credentials email
- Ensure you're clicking the most recent link (older links may expire)
- Try a different browser if you're having technical issues
- Contact support@stateaffairs.com for assistance

## Enhanced Content Features

Once logged in, you'll have access to:
- **Real-time News Updates**: Get stories as they're published
- **Keyword Alerts**: Set up custom alerts for topics you follow
- **Advanced Search**: Search our complete archive
- **Mobile Access**: Access content on any device
- **Multiple User Management**: Add team members to your account

## Managing Your Enhanced Content Experience

### Setting Up Keyword Alerts

1. Log in to your State Affairs account
2. Navigate to "Settings" or "Alerts"
3. Click "Add Keyword Alert"
4. Enter the terms or phrases you want to track
5. Choose your notification preferences
6. Save your settings

**Note:** You can set up alerts for:
- Specific legislators or officials
- Policy topics
- Organizations
- Geographic regions
- Bill numbers

### Keyword Alerts Not Working?

**Issue: Keywords disappeared or can't add new ones**

**Solution:**
1. Log out and log back in
2. Navigate to the Alerts section again
3. Follow the keyword setup prompts
4. If the issue persists, contact support@stateaffairs.com

**Issue: Stuck in a loop when setting up keywords**

**Solution:**
- Clear your browser cache
- Try a different browser
- Contact support for manual keyword setup

### Email Notification Preferences

**Want to receive ALL stories?**
- By default, you'll receive notifications for your keyword alerts
- To receive every story as it's published, contact support@stateaffairs.com
- We can adjust your notification settings

**Want fewer emails?**
- You can adjust notification frequency in your account settings
- Options include: Immediate, Daily Digest, Weekly Summary
- You can also disable email notifications and check the platform directly

## Adding Team Members

To add colleagues to your enhanced content account:

1. Contact support@stateaffairs.com with:
   - Names of team members to add
   - Their email addresses
   - Any specific access levels needed

2. We'll send them credential setup instructions
3. They'll follow the same activation process

**Note:** User limits depend on your subscription tier. Contact your account representative if you need to add more users than your plan allows.

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In

**Forgot Password:**
1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for reset instructions
4. Follow the link to create a new password

**Account Locked:**
- After multiple failed login attempts, accounts may be temporarily locked
- Wait 15 minutes and try again
- Or contact support@stateaffairs.com for immediate unlock

### Missing Content

If you're not seeing content you expect:
1. Verify your subscription status
2. Check if your payment is current
3. Clear your browser cache
4. Contact support@stateaffairs.com to verify your account access
