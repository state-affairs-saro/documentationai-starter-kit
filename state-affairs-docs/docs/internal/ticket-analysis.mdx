# Support Ticket Analysis

# HubSpot Support Ticket Analysis Report
**Date:** January 29, 2026  
**Total Tickets Analyzed:** 41 closed tickets in Support Pipeline  
**Analysis Period:** June 2025 - January 2026

---

## Executive Summary

This report analyzes 41 closed support tickets to identify common customer issues, response patterns, and opportunities for self-service content creation. The analysis reveals clear patterns in customer inquiries that can be addressed through comprehensive help center documentation.

---

## Ticket Category Breakdown

### 1. Billing/Invoice Issues (29% of tickets)
**Most Common:**
- Double billing or unexpected charges
- Questions about invoice validity
- Multiple subscription confusion
- Autopay issues
- Refund requests

**Key Insights:**
- Often involves system migration issues that caused autopay misconfigurations
- Customers frequently have multiple subscriptions without realizing it
- Requires detailed invoice breakdown and explanation
- Resolution typically involves subscription review and cancellation

**Average Resolution Time:** 3-5 email exchanges over 1-8 weeks

---

### 2. Access Issues (24% of tickets)
**Most Common:**
- Requesting enhanced content credentials
- Password resets
- Account activation
- Adding team members

**Key Insights:**
- Very straightforward to resolve
- Most resolved in 1-2 email exchanges
- Primary delay is email delivery (spam folders)
- High satisfaction when resolved quickly

**Average Resolution Time:** 1-2 email exchanges, same day to 24 hours

---

### 3. General Inquiries (19% of tickets)
**Most Common:**
- Feature questions (keyword alerts)
- Notification preferences
- Account settings
- Product usage questions

**Key Insights:**
- Often involves explaining how features work
- Some technical troubleshooting required
- Users want more control over notifications
- Many resolved with simple instructions

**Average Resolution Time:** 2-3 email exchanges, 1-2 days

---

### 4. Login/Password Issues (12% of tickets)
**Most Common:**
- Automated password reset notifications (often spam/irrelevant)
- Forgotten credentials
- Account lockouts

**Key Insights:**
- Many tickets in this category are automated notifications marked as spam
- Actual user-initiated password issues are rare
- Quick to resolve when legitimate

**Average Resolution Time:** Immediate for spam, 1 day for legitimate issues

---

### 5. Product Issues/Bugs (7% of tickets)
**Most Common:**
- Keyword alerts not working
- Features not appearing correctly
- System glitches

**Key Insights:**
- Usually resolved by logging out/in or clearing cache
- Some require manual backend fixes
- Often temporary issues that self-resolve

**Average Resolution Time:** 1-2 days

---

### 6. Delivery Issues (5% of tickets)
**Most Common:**
- Missing newspapers
- Duplicate deliveries
- Address changes

**Key Insights:**
- Requires coordination with printer
- Longer resolution time due to third-party involvement
- Can be recurring issue

**Average Resolution Time:** 1-2 weeks (due to print cycle)

---

### 7. Escalations (2% of tickets)
**Most Common:**
- Complaints about sales representatives
- Issues requiring management involvement

**Key Insights:**
- Handled by transferring to senior leadership
- Privacy-focused approach (closing tickets to protect conversation)
- Rare but important to handle properly

**Average Resolution Time:** Transferred immediately, ongoing resolution

---

### 8. Spam/Irrelevant (2% of tickets)
**Most Common:**
- Automated vendor notifications
- Misdirected emails

**Key Insights:**
- Properly identified and marked
- No action required

## Technical Implementation Notes

### Help Center Platform Options:

1. **HubSpot Knowledge Base** (Recommended)
   - Integrated with existing HubSpot setup
   - Easy for team to maintain
   - Good analytics
   - Searchable

2. **Dedicated Help Site**
   - More customization options
   - Better for complex documentation
   - Requires separate platform

3. **Integrated FAQ Page**
   - Quick to implement
   - Limited functionality
   - Good starting point

### Search Optimization:
- Tag all articles with common search terms
- Use customer language (not internal jargon)
- Cross-link related articles
- Implement "Related Articles" suggestions
