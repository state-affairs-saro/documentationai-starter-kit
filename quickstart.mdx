## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" title="What you'll need">
- A valid email address
- Access to your email inbox for verification
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- Optional: The State Affairs mobile app for iOS or Android
</Callout>

No software installation is required. You'll access everything through your web browser or mobile app.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

Sign up for State Affairs to gain access to 50-state legislative intelligence and AI-powered policy tracking.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the sign-up page" icon="external-link">
    Navigate to the [Get Started page](https://stateaffairs.com/get-started).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your details">
    Provide your email, create a password, and select your account type (individual or organization).

    ```
    Example:
    - Email: your.email@example.com
    - Password: StrongPassword123!
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit and agree to terms">
    Review the terms of service and privacy policy, then click **Create Account**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Verify Your Email

Check your inbox (and spam folder) for a verification email from State Affairs.

<Callout kind="tip">
  The verification link expires in 24 hours. Complete this step immediately after sign-up.
</Callout>

Click the link in the email to activate your account. You'll be redirected to the login page.

## Step 3: Log In to the Dashboard

Access your personalized dashboard at [pro.stateaffairs.com/login](https://pro.stateaffairs.com/login).

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web Browser" icon="globe">
    Enter your email and password, then click **Log In**.

    Once logged in, you'll see the main dashboard with recent policy alerts and search bar.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Mobile App" icon="smartphone">
    Download the app from the [App Store](https://stateaffairs.com/mobile-app) or Google Play.

    ```

    bash
    # Scan the QR code or search "State Affairs" in your app store
    ```

    Log in using the same credentials.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Step 4: Perform Your First Policy Search

Navigate the intuitive interface to start tracking legislation.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Use the search bar" icon="search">
    Type a keyword like `climate change funding` or select a state from the dropdown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter results">
    Apply filters for bill status, committee, or introduction date.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save alerts">
    Click **Track** on relevant bills to set up keyword alerts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Example search results include bill summaries, sponsor details, and AI-generated insights.

## Step 5: Set Up Basic Preferences

Customize your experience for better personalization.

<Expandable title="Notification Preferences" default-open="true">
  Go to **Settings > Notifications**.

  - Enable email alerts for tracked bills
  - Set daily/weekly digest frequency
  - Choose states to monitor (e.g., `California`, `Texas`, `New York`)
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Dashboard Layout">
  Drag and drop widgets for quick views:

  | Widget       | Purpose                     |
  |--------------|-----------------------------|
  | Alerts Feed  | Real-time bill updates      |
  | Keyword Track| Custom search monitoring    |
  | State Overview | 50-state legislative summary|
</Expandable>
