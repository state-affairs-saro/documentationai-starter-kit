## Overview

State Affairs delivers insider legislative intelligence at 50-state scale. You get AI-powered policy insights, personalized state news, and nonpartisan tracking designed for organizations, researchers, and policymakers who need to act first. Access real-time alerts, keyword monitoring, and comprehensive coverage to stay ahead of legislative changes.
