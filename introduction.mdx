## What is State Affairs?

State Affairs is a web-based platform where your organization can centralize and manage its documentation. After you log in with your organization email and password, you access a workspace or dashboard where you can view and work with projects and documents shared with your team.

## What you will find in these docs

These documentation pages are designed to help technically proficient users:

- Understand how the State Affairs workspace and dashboards are organized.
- Learn how to work with projects and documents.
- Troubleshoot common access or viewing issues.
- Find links to help center articles for account, billing, and access questions.
